Service/Volunteering

In my time at A&M, I have been involved in several service and volunteering opportunities. Last spring I participated in Big Event - the largest student run service project in the nation. I participated in Big Event as a sophomore in SAIL - where in addition to Big Event, we also ran other philanthropic activities like StriveK, a 5k race to raise funds for scholarships for high school students in the Bryan College Station community. As a part of Gents, our philanthropy is the Iron Freedom Foundation, a non-profit organization that serves veterans through wilderness therapy and fellowship. We raise money for the Iron Freedom Foundation through activies such as Songfest and Mr Alpha Chi.

Involvement/Activities

My involvement extends beyond the classroom, as I actively participate in several university organizations. I am a proud member of Sophomores Advancing In Leadership (SAIL), Gents of Texas A&M, and Fish Camp. Being a part of these organizations has brought me numerous opportunities to expand my network of Aggies as well as create lifelong memories. These affiliations not only contribute to my personal growth but also allow me to make meaningful connections with my peers. I think involvement outside of the classroom is absolutely crucial to developement and personability, and I believe these experiences will serve me well in the workplace.
